In season now

Wild Garlic

(Ramsløg)

Found in moist deciduous forests. Smells strongly of garlic. Watch for toxic Lily of the Valley.

Stinging Nettle

(Brændenælde)

A nutritional powerhouse. Harvest top shoots in spring. Always wear gloves when picking.

Ground Elder

(Skvalderkål)

Pick when leaves are shiny and "folded" for best flavor. Tastes like a mix of celery and parsley.
