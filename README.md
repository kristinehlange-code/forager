# Forager

A mobile foraging companion for the Danish landscape, built as a personal reference tool for two friends learning to forage together.

## What it does

- **Field Guide** — plant cards with identification tips, lookalike warnings, and seasonal dot indicators showing exactly when each plant is harvestable
- **Kitchen** — foraged recipes organised by season and type (fresh, preserve, drink)
- **Month detail** — a deep dive into each month of the foraging year, with focus plants, field notes, and preservation tips
- **My Log** — a personal walk diary with GPS location pins, voice-to-text notes, and photos taken directly from your phone camera

## How to use it

Visit the site on your phone and add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

On iPhone: open in Safari, tap the share icon, tap Add to Home Screen.
On Android: open in Chrome, tap the three dots menu, tap Add to Home Screen.

## Status

The site is working and navigable. Plant and recipe detail pages are in progress. Full plant content from a Danish foraging course will be added in a future update.

## Built with

HTML, Tailwind CSS, Lucide icons, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools. Five self-contained HTML files.
